Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich consists of peanut butter and fruit preserves—jelly—spread on bread.

The sandwich may be open-faced, made of a single slice of bread folded over, or made using two slices of bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon of jelly
  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter

Steps

  • Smear jelly on one side of bread
  • Smear peanut butter on the other slice of bread
  • Connect two slices together
  • Enjoy
